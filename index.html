Workshops

SARC presents to IITB students, a series of conducive, certified workshops on a panorama of coveted topics like consult, finance, analytics, and entrepreneurship. These lucrative workshops will comprehensively infuse to the core, all foundational and critical concepts, helping you to meliorate your skills and refine your competitive edge to prepare you for the forthcoming battles.











Mock Interviews

Whether you want to work in the core or non-core sectors, or maybe go for higher studies, you will have to face one common hurdle: interviews. SARC presents to you Mock interviews conducted by professionals from various industries that will help you get familiar with interviews in the best way possible.












Alumination

The 'Piece de Resistance' of SARC, Alumination is IIT Bombay's largest platform for bilateral exchange between the IITB alumni and students on a spectrum spanning Career Guidance, Corporate Exposure, Life Learning and Motivational Talks.
